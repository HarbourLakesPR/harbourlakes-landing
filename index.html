On-Site Storm Preparedness

  • Deploy accordion or panel hurricane shutters across patio openings completely.
  • Clear outdoor terrace decks of any loose furniture, planters, or lightweight objects.
  • Ensure the auxiliary backup cistern power switch is operational for family water security.
  • Monitor the regional status notifications pushed out on the IsMyNest community app.
